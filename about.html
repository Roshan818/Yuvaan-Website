Who Are We?

Team Yuvaan IITG is a student team of IIT Guwahati. We are a dynamic team of engineering undergrads who have come together to demonstrate technical, designing and management skill to compete in International Rover Challenge(IRC) which gets participation from around the globe. The competetion tests the capabilities of the rover with respect to advanced robotic software and autonomous navigation for extra-terrestial space exploration. We work together at IIT Guwahati's Robotics club with our common goal to make the best Rover with cutting edge technology.

What We Do?

Team Yuvaan debuted in IRDC 2021 and achieved 16th rank overall internationally among 32 teams. We are currently working on our second bot preparing for IRC 2021.

In future, our team also aspires to participate in URC(University Rover Challenge) and other international design and innovation challenges.
