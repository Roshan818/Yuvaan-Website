About Us

Team YUVAAN is a group of passionate and like minded undergraduate students of IIT Guwahati working under the umbrella of extra-terrestial space exploration. The team comprises of students from all majors collectively aiming to develop cutting edge technology under extra-terestial space exploration and subsequently implement them in various globally acclaimed competetions.

Overview

Team Yuvaan is a robotic initiative of IIT Guwahati working on the field of Mars rover and is currently aiming to develop cutting edge technology for the same. The team is majorly divided into four subsystem for analysing and tackling different types of situations and problems which are present on Mars.

Mechanical Module

  • Suspension & Differential
  • Chassis
  • Manipulator
  • Wheels

Electronics Module

  • Control System
  • Navigation
  • Power Syatem
  • Vision

Artificial Intelligence

  • Automation
  • Symbol Detection
  • Software Control

Bio-Assembly Module

  • Life-Detection Test
  • Sample Collection
  • Bio-Sensers
  • Geological Survey
